VERITY

Mission:
Help people make trusted decisions using evidence instead of influencer opinions.

Vision:
Become the world's most trusted platform for verifying online claims.

Target Users:
- Gym enthusiasts
- Nutrition learners
- Skincare users

Problem:
People are confused because influencers give conflicting advice.

Solution:
An AI-powered trust engine that verifies claims using scientific evidence and trusted sources.

Version 1 (MVP):
- Analyze fitness claims
- Analyze nutrition claims
- Analyze skincare claims
- Show confidence score
- Show research sources
- Give a simple verdict
